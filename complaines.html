HIPAA Compliance and Data Privacy Protection

We go the extra mile to safeguard patient and customer data, adhering strictly to HIPAA regulations. Our team undergoes comprehensive training on HIPAA and privacy policies, supplemented by routine assessments to uphold stringent standards.

Standardized Governance and Regulatory Compliance

With a centralized governance structure, we've established cohesive policies to drive consistency and regulatory adherence throughout our organization. Events like Corporate Compliance and Ethics Week further reinforce our dedication to fostering a culture of compliance among our staff.

Third-Party Risk Management

Our proactive approach extends to evaluating third-party applications, mitigating potential risks through rigorous assessments. Each vendor undergoes thorough scrutiny via intensive information security reviews and annual evaluations. Furthermore, we closely monitor emerging security concerns related to artificial intelligence (AI).

Robust Information Security Practices

MedAssist Scribes prioritizes the implementation of established security frameworks and best practices across all solutions. Our Governance, Risk & Compliance Team actively seeks and maintains certifications from reputable organizations, ensuring swift responses to security inquiries.

Recognized Certifications

We are proud recipients of the HITRUST i1 certification for our eSOne™U.S. Platform, underscoring our commitment to excellence in healthcare. Additionally, as members of StateRAMP in participating states, we actively support cybersecurity best practices through advocacy and education initiatives.
